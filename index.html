Ingredients

  • slices Dave's Killer Bread
  • ½ Avocado - sliced
  • ¼ cup parmesan cheese -shredded
  • Everything bagel seasoning for to taste
  • ½ cup cottage cheese (full fat, it's really the best)
  • ¼ cup green onion, chopped
  • cup of whole blueberries

instructions

  1. Fry eggs as desired
  2. Toast the bread to liking
  3. Spread avocado on bread; add bagel seasoning
  4. Add 1 egg to each slice; top with parmesan and green onion
  5. Add cottage cheese and blueberries to the side
